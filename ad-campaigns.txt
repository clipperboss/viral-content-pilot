AD CAMPAIGN TRACKING & OPTIMIZATION
========================================

KPIs TO TRACK:
‚úì Cost Per Lead (Target: $20-$50)
‚úì Landing Page Conversion Rate (Target: 25-40%)
‚úì Call Booking Rate (Target: 15-25%)
‚úì Call Show Rate (Target: 60-70%)
‚úì Call-to-Close Rate (Target: 20-40%)
‚úì ROAS (Target: 6X minimum, 12X ideal)

OPTIMIZATION SCHEDULE:
- Day 1-3: Let ads gather data (no changes)
- Day 4-7: Turn off worst-performing ads (bottom 20%)
- Week 2: Duplicate winning ads, test new angles
- Week 3: Scale budget on winners by 20%
- Week 4: Introduce new ad creative variations

WINNING AD FORMULA:
1. Hook (3-5 seconds): Pattern interrupt that speaks to target pain
2. Problem (10-15 seconds): Agitate current situation
3. Solution (10-15 seconds): Introduce the funnel system
4. Proof (5-10 seconds): Results, credentials, social proof
5. CTA (5 seconds): Clear next step with urgency

RETARGETING STRATEGY:
- Create custom audience: Website visitors (last 30 days)
- Create custom audience: Video viewers (50%+ watched)
- Create custom audience: Landing page visitors (didn't opt-in)
- Retarget with more aggressive CTAs and limited spots language
